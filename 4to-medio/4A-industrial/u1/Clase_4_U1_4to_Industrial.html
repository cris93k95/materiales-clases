🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Aplicar estrategias de comunicación oral para responder preguntas frecuentes de entrevista laboral en inglés en el contexto de Mecánica Industrial.

🟢 Inicio (15 min)
💬 Warm-up: "Have you ever had a job interview? What questions did they ask?"

The 3 phases: Introduction → Questions & Answers → Closing

🔵 Desarrollo (60 min)

📖 Activity 1 — Common Interview Questions (15 min)

General Questions

Q: "Tell me about yourself."

Q: "Why do you want this job?"

Q: "What are your strengths and weaknesses?"

Technical Questions for Industrial Mechanics

Q: "What CNC machines have you operated?"

Model: "I have operated CNC lathes and milling machines, including programming..."

Q: "How do you ensure quality in your work?"

Model: "I use precision measurement tools like calipers and micrometers to..."

Q: "What welding techniques are you certified in?"

Model: "I am trained in both MIG and TIG welding and always follow..."

🗣️ Activity 3 — Role-Play (25 min)

In pairs: one = interviewer, one = candidate. Use your CV as reference. Take turns.

💡 Interview Tips:

✅ Make eye contact · ✅ Say "I believe..." instead of "I think..."

✅ Don't say "I don't know" → say "I am learning about..."

✅ Always thank the interviewer at the end

🟣 Cierre (15 min)

📝 Best Answer: "Tell me about yourself"
