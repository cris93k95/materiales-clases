✏️ Activity 4 — Build Your Professional CV (25 min)

📝 Writing Tips:
  • Use a professional email: firstname.lastname@email.com
  • Write your objective using: "To obtain a position as a... where I can apply my... skills"
  • Use action verbs for experience: Diagnosed, Repaired, Replaced, Tested, Inspected, Serviced
  • Be specific with skills — avoid "good with cars"
