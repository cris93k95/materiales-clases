📖 Activity 1 — Reading: Safety Protocols for Automotive Mechanics (20 min)

Rule 1: Jack stand safety

Always use jack stands when working under a vehicle. Never rely on a hydraulic jack alone.

Rule 2: Chemical handling

Wear chemical-resistant gloves when handling coolant, brake fluid, or oil. These can irritate skin.

Rule 3: Battery safety

Disconnect the negative terminal first. Never smoke or create sparks near batteries — hydrogen gas is explosive.

Rule 4: Lifting procedures

Use proper lifting technique (bend knees, keep back straight) when lifting heavy parts like transmissions.
