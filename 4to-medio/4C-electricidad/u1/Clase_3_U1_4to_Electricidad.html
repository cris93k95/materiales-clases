✏️ Activity 4 — Build Your Professional CV (25 min)

📝 Writing Tips:
  • Use a professional email: firstname.lastname@email.com
  • Write your objective using: "To obtain a position as a... where I can apply my... skills"
  • Use action verbs for experience: Installed, Tested, Wired, Connected, Inspected, Maintained
  • Be specific with skills — avoid "good with electricity"
⚡ Remember: Electrical work requires safety knowledge. Mentioning SEC regulations and safety training in your CV shows professionalism!
