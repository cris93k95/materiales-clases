CLASS 2 — 4°C — ELECTRICIDAD

📘 OA1 · OA3 ⏱ 90 min 🧭 Guía de estudiante completa

🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Analizar un estudio de caso sobre la trayectoria profesional de un técnico de Electricidad, identificando hitos clave, habilidades requeridas y el rol del inglés en su carrera.

Inicio (15 min)
  • Warm-up: repaso de vocabulario con "Word Relay" — en equipos, cada estudiante dice una palabra técnica en inglés; el equipo con más palabras gana.
  • Pre-reading: "What steps does someone take to become a successful professional in electricidad?" — lluvia de ideas.
  • Se introduce el concepto de Case Study como herramienta de aprendizaje.
Desarrollo (60 min)
  • Reading (25 min): Case Study: Powering a Career — María, a Chilean electrician, shares her career journey: technical school, apprenticeship in residential wiring, specialization in solar panel installation, and starting her own business. She uses English to read international electrical codes. Lectura guiada con pausas. Los estudiantes completan una tabla: Stage (education, internship, first job, specialization) Skills developed at each stage Role of English at each stage Challenges faced
  • Stage (education, internship, first job, specialization)
  • Skills developed at each stage
  • Role of English at each stage
  • Challenges faced
  • Comprehension (15 min): 8 preguntas: 4 explícitas + 4 de análisis crítico ( "Why was English important for his/her career?" , "What would you do differently?" ).

📚 Activity 2 — Reading

Rocío built her career through internships in residential and industrial electrical projects.

She learned load calculation, transformer maintenance, and protection system testing.

English was key to understanding electrical codes and technical documentation.

📝 Activity 3 — Comprehension Questions

  1. What is the main job/task described in the text?
  2. Which technical tools or systems are mentioned?
  3. What sequence of actions appears in the routine?
  4. Why is English useful in this professional context?
  5. What challenge does the professional face and how is it solved?
  6. What skill from this text is relevant for your own future career?
