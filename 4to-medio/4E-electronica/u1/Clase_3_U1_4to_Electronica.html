WORK EXPERIENCE
Internship — TechSur Electronics (January–February 2026)
• Assembled and tested prototype circuit boards
• Programmed Arduino-based sensors for automated systems

TECHNICAL SKILLS
• Circuit board assembly and repair
• Microcontroller programming (Arduino, ESP32)
• PCB design and layout (KiCad)
• Component soldering (SMD and through-hole)

• IoT device configuration
• Signal testing and analysis

• Analytical thinking · Teamwork · Attention to detail · Creativity

Spanish (Native) · English (Basic-Intermediate)

✏️ Activity 4 — Build Your Professional CV (25 min)

📝 Writing Tips:
  • Use a professional email: firstname.lastname@email.com
  • Write your objective using: "To obtain a position as a... where I can apply my... skills"
  • Use action verbs for experience: Assembled, Programmed, Tested, Soldered, Designed, Configured
  • Be specific with skills — mention tools and platforms (Arduino, KiCad, etc.)
