📚 Activity 3: Reading — "A Day in an Industrial Workshop"

⚙️ A Day in an Industrial Workshop

My name is Diego, and I work in an industrial workshop. In the morning, I check the blueprint for today’s project. Then I use the lathe to shape a metal part.

After that, I measure the piece with a caliper and a micrometer to verify the tolerance. If the size is correct, I continue with the next step.

Later, I use the welder to join two steel components. Finally, I clean the surface with a file and inspect the final product.

In this job, reading instructions in English is very important for safety and quality.
