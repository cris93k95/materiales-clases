📚 Activity 3: Reading — "A Day as an Electrician" (15 min)

Lee el siguiente texto. Las palabras destacadas son del vocabulario de hoy. Subráyalas en tu ficha.

⚡ A Day as an Electrician

My name is Carlos. I am an electrician. I work in Santiago, Chile. Every morning, I check my tools: my multimeter, my pliers, and my safety equipment.

At 8:00 AM, I go to the construction site. Today, I need to install new wires in the building. First, I put the wires inside the conduit on the wall. Then, I connect them to the electrical panel.

After lunch, I check the circuits. I use my multimeter to measure the voltage and the current. Everything looks good. The resistance is correct.

At 3:00 PM, I install a new breaker in the panel. I also install three outlets and two switches in the rooms. Finally, I check the grounding connection. Safety is very important!

I finish work at 5:00 PM. I like my job because I help people have electricity in their homes. Being an electrician is a great career!
