📖 Activity 1 — Reading: Safety in Electrical Work (25 min)

Safety in Electrical Work

Rule 1: Always de-energize circuits before working. Reason: Prevents electrical shock and electrocution. Equipment: Voltage tester.

Rule 2: Use insulated tools. Reason: Protects from accidental contact with live wires. Equipment: Insulated screwdrivers.

Rule 3: Never work alone on high-voltage systems. Reason: A partner can call for help in emergencies. Equipment: Communication radio.

Rule 4: Wear rubber-soled boots. Reason: Provides insulation from ground. Equipment: Safety boots.

Rule 5: Lock out / Tag out (LOTO). Reason: Prevents someone from turning on power while you work. Equipment: LOTO padlock.
