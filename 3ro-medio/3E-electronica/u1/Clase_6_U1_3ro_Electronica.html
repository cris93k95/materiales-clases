📖 Activity 1 — Reading: Safety in the Electronics Lab (25 min)

Safety in the Electronics Lab

Rule 1: Wear an anti-static wrist strap. Reason: Prevents static electricity from damaging components. Equipment: ESD wrist strap.

Rule 2: Use proper ventilation when soldering. Reason: Solder fumes can be harmful. Equipment: Fume extractor.

Rule 3: Never touch components while powered on. Reason: Even low voltage can damage components and cause burns. Equipment: Insulated tools.

Rule 4: Keep liquids away from the workbench. Reason: Spills can short-circuit boards. Equipment: Sealed containers.

Rule 5: Handle capacitors with care. Reason: Large capacitors can store dangerous charge even when unplugged. Equipment: Discharge tool.
