📖 Activity 2 — New Reading (15 min)

Innovation in Graphic Arts

The world of graphic arts is changing rapidly. New technologies like AI, automation, and digital tools are transforming how professionals work. For example, many tasks that used to be done manually are now done with the help of computers and specialized software.

English remains essential because most technical documentation, software interfaces, and international training programs are in English. Professionals who can read and communicate in English have better career opportunities and can access the latest information in their field.

1. What is changing in graphic arts?

2. Why is English important for new technologies?
