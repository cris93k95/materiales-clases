📚 Activity 3: Reading — "A Day in a Print Shop" (15 min)

Lee el siguiente texto. Las palabras destacadas son del vocabulario de hoy. Subráyalas en tu ficha.

🖨️ A Day in a Print Shop

My name is Valentina. I work in a print shop in Talca, Chile. Every morning, I start my day in the prepress area. I check the files on the computer. The images need good resolution — at least 300 DPI.

At 9:00 AM, I prepare the color profile. Our machines use CMYK colors: cyan, magenta, yellow, and black. I make sure the colors look correct on the screen and on paper.

At 10:00 AM, we start printing. I put the ink in the press. Today we use the offset machine for 500 flyers. I also use the densitometer to check the ink quality on the plate.

After lunch, I work on finishing. First, I use the cutter to cut the paper to the correct size. Then, I use the laminator to add a protective layer to some covers. Finally, I do the binding of a set of booklets.

I love my job! Digital printing is faster, but offset gives the best quality for large orders. Being a graphic arts technician is a creative and technical career!
