📖 Section II — Reading Comprehension (30 pts)

The New Technician

Sofía has just started working at a technical workshop in Temuco. She has studied electronics for three years at a technical school. She has built more than 20 circuit boards and has repaired many devices.

On her first day, the supervisor gives her a broken radio to fix. Sofía opens the radio and inspects the circuit board. She finds a burnt resistor and a loose wire. She uses her soldering iron to replace the resistor and reconnects the wire.

After fixing the radio, Sofía tests it. It works perfectly. The supervisor is impressed. He says: "You have done a great job. We used to send radios like this to another shop, but now we can fix them here."

Sofía is happy. She has wanted to work in electronics since she was 12 years old. She used to take apart her family's old phones and radios to see how they worked. Now, she does it professionally.

1. How long has Sofía studied electronics?

2. What was wrong with the radio?

3. What tool did Sofía use to fix the radio?

4. What did the workshop 'used to' do with broken radios?

5. How long has Sofía wanted to work in electronics?
