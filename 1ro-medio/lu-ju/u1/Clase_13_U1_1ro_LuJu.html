CLASS 13 — 1° MEDIO — INGLÉS GENERAL (LU+JU)

Unidad 1 — The World of Technical Work

📘 OA5 · OA8 ⏱ 90 min 🧭 Guía de estudiante completa

🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Describir oralmente un proceso técnico simple utilizando vocabulario especializado y conectores de secuencia.

🟢 Inicio (15 min)
📖 Sequence Connectors Review

first → second → then → next → after that → finally

🗣️ Teacher Model: Listen to the teacher describe a process step by step: "How to change a tire." Take notes.

"How many steps were there?"

🔵 Desarrollo (60 min)

🎬 Activity 1 — Watch & Note (15 min)

Watch a short video of a technical process. Take notes on the steps.

"What did he/she do first? Then what?"

StepWhat happened

✏️ Activity 2 — Write Your Process (20 min)

In pairs, choose a technical process and write 5-7 steps using sequence connectors:

Choose one: How to check a car battery · How to measure voltage with a multimeter · How to use a soldering iron · How to prepare a file for printing · How to use a bench vise
🗣️ Activity 3 — Oral Practice (25 min)

Practice explaining your process orally (without reading!). Then join another pair:

1. Explain your process to the other pair.

2. They must guess which specialty your process belongs to.

3. They can ask one question.

🟣 Cierre (15 min)
🗣️ Volunteer Presentations: 2-3 pairs present their process to the class.

"Was the explanation clear? What vocabulary did they use well?"
