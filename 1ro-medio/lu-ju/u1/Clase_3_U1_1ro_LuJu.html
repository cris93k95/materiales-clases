📖 Activity 2 — Reading: A Day at González Automotive Workshop (25 min)

A Day at González Automotive Workshop

Luis González owns a small automotive workshop in Rancagua, Chile. Today, a customer brings in a 2018 sedan. The engine is making strange noises and the car is losing power.

Luis starts with a visual inspection. He opens the hood and checks the engine. He notices the oil is very dark and dirty — the oil filter needs to be replaced. He also checks the spark plugs. Two of them are worn out.

Next, Luis connects a diagnostic scanner to the car's computer. The scanner shows an error code related to the exhaust system. Luis inspects the exhaust pipe and finds a small crack.

Luis explains the problems to the customer: "You need a new oil filter, two spark plugs, and a repair on the exhaust pipe." The customer agrees.

Luis changes the oil filter first, using a wrench and an oil pan. Then he replaces the two spark plugs with a socket set. Finally, he welds the crack in the exhaust pipe.

After finishing, Luis starts the engine. It runs smoothly — no more strange noises. The customer is happy and pays the bill. Luis writes a service report and cleans his tools. Another successful day at the workshop!
