CLASS 9 — 1° MEDIO — INGLÉS GENERAL (LU+JU)

Unidad 1 — The World of Technical Work

📘 OA8 ⏱ 90 min 🧭 Guía de estudiante completa

🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Comprender la estructura y el uso del Present Perfect Simple para describir logros y experiencias en contextos técnico-profesionales.

🟢 Inicio (15 min)
🤔 Compare these sentences:

"I have repaired 50 cars." vs. "I repaired a car yesterday."

What is the difference? When do we use each one?

🔵 Desarrollo (60 min)

📖 Activity 1 — Grammar: Present Perfect Simple (20 min)

Structure: Subject + have/has + past participle

Uses:

✅ Experiences: "Have you ever welded?"

✅ Achievements: "She has installed 200 panels."

✅ Ongoing situations: "He has worked here for 10 years."

Examples from each specialty:

🔧 "She has repaired many engines."

⚙️ "I have welded 100 structures."

⚡ "We have installed the wiring."

🔌 "They have built 30 circuits."

🎨 "He has designed a new logo."

✏️ Activity 2 — Gap-Fill Practice (15 min)

Complete with the correct Present Perfect form:

1. "She _____ (repair) over 200 engines in her career."

2. "They _____ (build) a new circuit board."

3. "_____ you ever _____ (use) a soldering iron?"

4. "He _____ never _____ (work) in a print shop."

📖 Activity 3 — Reading: Interview with Don Pedro (25 min)

An Experienced Mechanic: Interview with Don Pedro

Reporter: Don Pedro, how long have you worked as a mechanic?

Don Pedro: I have worked in this workshop for 30 years. I started when I was 18.

Reporter: What have you done in your career?

Don Pedro: Oh, many things! I have repaired thousands of cars. I have changed engines, fixed transmissions, and replaced brakes. I have also trained more than 50 young apprentices.

Reporter: Have you ever had a difficult moment?

Don Pedro: Yes, I have faced many challenges. Once, a customer brought a car that nobody could fix. I worked on it for two weeks. I have never forgotten that car — it was my biggest challenge. But I fixed it!

Reporter: Have you ever had an accident?

Don Pedro: I have always been very careful. I have never had a serious accident because I always follow safety rules. But I have seen other mechanics get hurt because they didn't wear their PPE.

Reporter: What advice do you have for young technicians?

Don Pedro: Learn English! I have read many manuals in English throughout my career. And always wear your safety equipment.

✅ Activity 4 — Comprehension

5. How long has Don Pedro worked as a mechanic?

6. Has Don Pedro ever had a serious accident?

7. What advice does Don Pedro give?

🟣 Cierre (15 min)
🗣️ Oral Round: Each student says one sentence using Present Perfect related to learning:

"I have learned about electrical circuits." / "I have never used a lathe."
