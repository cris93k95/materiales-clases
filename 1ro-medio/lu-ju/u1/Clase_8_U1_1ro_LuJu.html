Unidad 1 — The World of Technical Work

📘 OA8 · OA9 · OA5 ⏱ 90 min 🧭 Guía de estudiante completa

🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Sintetizar y aplicar el vocabulario técnico de las cinco especialidades en actividades integradas de comprensión lectora y producción oral.

🟢 Inicio (15 min)

🏁 Vocabulary Relay Race

Competition: Teams of 5 compete. Images of tools are projected — the first team to identify correctly wins a point!

"We have explored 5 specialties. Today we bring them all together."

📖 Activity 2 — Reading: Five Careers, Five Futures (25 min)

Five Careers, Five Futures

Pedro (Automotive): "I repair engines and change brakes every day. I use a wrench, a scanner, and a jack. I love solving problems — every car is a puzzle!"

Javiera (Industrial): "I operate a lathe and a milling machine. I make metal parts for mining equipment. I enjoy working with heavy machines and precision tools like calipers."

Sebastián (Electricity): "I install wiring and fix circuits. I always carry my multimeter and wire cutters. I like that my work makes buildings safe and functional."

Isidora (Electronics): "I build circuit boards and repair electronic devices. My favorite tool is the soldering iron. I love technology and learning about new microcontrollers."

Martín (Graphic Arts): "I operate the printing press and check color proofs. I work with digital files, ink, and different types of paper. I like creating something physical from a digital design."

🗣️ Activity 3 — Role Play (15 min)

In groups of 5, each student "represents" a different specialty. You have 1 minute to explain what you do and why you like it. Others can ask 1 question.

🟣 Cierre (15 min)
💬 Reflection: "Which specialty vocabulary was easiest for you? Which was the hardest? Why?"
