Unidad 1 — The World of Technical Work

📘 OA8 · OA5 ⏱ 90 minutos 🧭 Material de clase

🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Diferenciar el uso de "since" y "for" con Present Perfect para describir la duración de actividades técnico-profesionales.

Class focus: active vocabulary + guided practice + exit ticket.
🟢 Inicio (15 min)
  • Repaso: "What have you learned so far in English class?" Los estudiantes responden usando Present Perfect.
  • Escribir: "I have studied English FOR 8 years." y "I have studied English SINCE 2018."
  • Preguntar: "Both sentences are correct. What is the difference between FOR and SINCE?" Recoger respuestas.
🔵 Desarrollo (60 min)

📚 Reading Organizer — He has worked at the factory since 2015.

Before reading: What do you think this text is about?

While reading: Underline key words and verbs.

After reading: Write 3 key ideas from the text.

🟣 Cierre (15 min)
  • Escribir 2 oraciones sobre sí mismos usando since y for . Compartir con la clase.
  • Quick quiz oral: el docente dice una expresión de tiempo y los estudiantes dicen si va con since o for : "2020" → since. "Three hours" → for. "Last Monday" → since.
