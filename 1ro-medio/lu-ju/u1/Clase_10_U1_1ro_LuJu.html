Unidad 1 — The World of Technical Work

📘 OA8 · OA5 ⏱ 90 min 🧭 Guía de estudiante completa

🎯 Objetivo de la clase

Diferenciar el uso de 'since' y 'for' con Present Perfect para describir la duración de actividades técnico-profesionales.

🟢 Inicio (15 min)
🤔 Compare:

"I have studied English FOR 8 years."

"I have studied English SINCE 2018."

Both are correct. What is the difference?

🔵 Desarrollo (60 min)

📖 Activity 1 — Grammar: Since vs For (15 min)

SINCE = a specific point in time (2015, Monday, January, I was 10)

"He has worked at the factory since 2015."

FOR = a duration of time (3 years, 2 months, a long time)

"He has worked at the factory for 10 years."

✏️ Activity 2 — Since or For? (15 min)

1. "She has worked here _____ 2019."

2. "They have used this machine _____ 5 years."

3. "He has been a technician _____ he was 20."

4. "We have lived in Chile _____ a long time."

5. "This software has existed _____ 2010."
